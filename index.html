企业概况 Company Info


宁波鸿鹄电机有限公司始建于2000年，前身为“宁波中强电动工具集团”控股分公司。 公司秉承“真诚、求实、开拓、创新”的企业理念，根据市场的不同需求，设计，开发，生产各类永磁直流电机,串激电机及其他特种电机。 经过近二十多年不懈努力，发展成集生产、经营、科研于一体的电机生产企业。

本公司生产的各类电机产品均达到并超过标准，以“质量、稳定耐用”的特点而畅销国内，产品同时还远销美国、加拿大、意大利、韩国、中东、台湾等国家和地区（有部分产品随机配套出口世界各地），被广泛应用于电动工具、园林工具、台式工具、家用电器、机械设备、自动化办公设备、运动器械、医疗器械、农业机械等领域，深受用户好评。

面对日益激烈的市场竟争,本公司全体员工将一如既往地严格贯彻执行质量控制体系，继续以现代企业的管理理念，秉承以“以人为本,科技兴厂”的企业宗旨，充分致力于电机行业的创新，以满足客户的不同需求，充分赢得客户的信赖！ 公司将竭诚为社会提供较好的产品质量和服务，并衷心欢迎国内外新老客户光临我司洽淡商贸合作事宜！！


Ningbo Ambition Motor Co., Ltd. was founded in 2000, formerly known as the holding branch of "Ningbo Zhongqiang Electric Tool Group".

The company adheres to the corporate philosophy of "sincerity, truth-seeking, pioneering, and innovative", and designs, develops, and produces various types of permanent magnet DC motors, series motors and other special motors according to the different needs of the market. After nearly two decades of unremitting efforts, it has finally developed into a motor manufacturer integrating production, management and scientific research.

All kinds of motor products produced by our company have reached and exceeded the standard. They are sold well in China with the characteristics of "quality, stability and durability". The products are also exported to the United States, Italy, South Korea, the Middle East, Taiwan and other regions (some products are randomly equipped Exported all over the world), are widely used in power tools, garden tools, desktop tools, household appliances, mechanical equipment, automated office equipment, sports medical equipment, agricultural machinery and other fields, and are well received by users.

In the face of increasingly fierce market competition, all employees of the company will, as always, strictly implement the quality control system, continue to adopt the modern enterprise management philosophy, uphold the corporate tenet of "people-oriented, technology-based factory", and fully commit to the electrical machinery industry. Innovate to meet the different needs of customers and fully win the trust of customers! The company will wholeheartedly provide the society with better product quality and service, and sincerely welcome new and old customers at home and abroad to visit our company to discuss business cooperation matters! !
